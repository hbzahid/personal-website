About Me

Hello! This is Haris. I'm a Student Software Engineer studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo.

I have an ardent passion for programming which stems from an immense fondness for Mathematics. The most appealing aspect of computer science for me is its ability to bring numbers to life, to bridge the gap between abstract theoretical math and real world problems! For this reason, I have a natural inclination to study topics that are at the core of modern computer science, such as Algorithms, Discrete Math and Artificial Intelligence. My goal is to stay on this lifelong learning trajectory for as long as I can, and use my expertise to tackle problems that affect us in our everyday lives.

Outside of programming, I thoroughly enjoy following and playing the game of cricket. I also avidly read literature beyond academics, with The Power of Habit being my favorite book so far.
